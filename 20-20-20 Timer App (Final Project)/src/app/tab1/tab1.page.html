This mobile application aims to help users reduce eye strain while working in front of a screen via the 20/20/20 method. More specifically, this hybrid app provides users with two different timers: an active timer, which runs for 20 minutes, and a rest timer, which runs for 20 seconds. During the active period, users are free to work in front of their screens uninterrupted; during the rest period, users are encouraged to look at an object or area 20 feet away from them to give their eyes a break before returning to work. To begin the active period, click the Active Timer tab; to begin the rest period, click the Rest Timer tab. Use the Eye Profile tab to create a new Eye Profile. With an Eye Profile, you can store valuable medical information such as contacts and eyeglass prescriptions.
